When you have your guitar, press the tune button to open the tuning screen. There you can tune your strings and the note to be played when 'fretting' a particular key on your keyboard. When fretting, you only have to press a key once to change the key that's being fretted on a particular string. That being said, you must press the key that corresponds with a string's root note to stop fretting a note.
